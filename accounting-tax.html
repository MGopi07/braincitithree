Accounting and Bookkeeping Policies in Dubai

The UAE follows GAAP principles maintained by the International Federation of Accountants (IFAC). Below are the key policies every business owner must follow:

Record Keeping Policy

Businesses must keep financial records for at least 5 years. The Ministry of Finance recommends 10 years for tax compliance.

Cash Receipts

Transactions of AED 1,000+ must have a receipt with business name, date, signature, recipient, amount, and nature of transaction.

Bank Transactions

All bank transactions must be supported by a statement with date, business details, amount, and purpose. Electronic records are acceptable.

VAT Returns

Since Jan 1, 2018, VAT is mandatory. Businesses must register if eligible and follow a VAT Return Policy aligned with best practices.

Other Supporting Documents

Invoices, credit notes, contracts, and bank statements must be kept in their original form. Copies are not acceptable.
