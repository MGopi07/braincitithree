Company Formation & Trade License

Establish your business in the UAE with expert assistance. We provide complete support for obtaining trade licenses and registering your company in Free Zones, Mainland, or Offshore jurisdictions.

Visa & Immigration Services

Get professional visa processing for investors, employees, and dependents. We manage residency visas, employment permits, golden visas, and more – fast and hassle-free.

Document Attestation

We help you attest personal, educational, and commercial documents for official use in the UAE. Our services cover MOFA, embassy, and notary attestation requirements.

PRO Services

Banking Solutions

Open business or personal bank accounts with leading UAE banks. We assist with documentation, KYC compliance, and relationship management for smooth banking.

Trademark Registration

Protect your brand identity legally. We handle trademark searches, registration, renewal, and enforcement services for individuals and businesses across the UAE.
