Golden Visa Services in Dubai

Dubai’s Golden Visa offers long-term residency to investors, entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, and outstanding talents, giving you and your family the freedom to live, work, and study in the UAE without a local sponsor. With Brainciti, we simplify the process and handle all the requirements, ensuring a smooth, hassle-free experience.

Enjoy exclusive privileges for you and your family

Steps to Get the Dubai Golden Visa

Step 1: Check Eligibility – Investor, Entrepreneur, Professional, Student, or Talent.
Step 2: Gather Documents – Passport, Emirates ID, academic and property proof.
Step 3: Submit Application – Official channels or with Brainciti’s support.
Step 4: Medical Examination & Biometrics.
Step 5: Application Review & Approval.
Step 6: Golden Visa Issuance – 10-year renewable residency visa.
